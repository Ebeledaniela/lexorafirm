Protection Order case (August 2023)

In August 2023, we assisted a client who was experiencing repeated threats, harassment, and emotional distress.
The situation required urgent legal intervention to ensure their immediate safety and long-term protection.

Case Overview

The client reported ongoing harassment from a former partener.
The individual felt unsafe at home and in public spaces and there was no need for fast legal action to prevent further harm.

Approach

The court filed for an emergency protection order in court and presented evidence of harassment and threats.
The court requested restrictions preventing the offenderfrom contacting or approaching the client.

Result

The court granted a full Protection order, preventing the offender from making contact,
appearing near the client's home, workplace, or family members. This legal decision restored the client's peace of mind and provided protection under the law.
